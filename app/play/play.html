About Me

Short Biograpy

I have always been fascinated with technology. When I was younger I'd love to take things apart just to see what made them tick. Growing up, my first job was in IT working for my high school, where I ran tech support and some basic server management. From there, I knew I wanted to work with information technology. So far in my college career, I've worked on several projects with small teams, some of which you can view on my site under projects. I am a fast learner, with a passion for programming and problem solving.

I always believe in picking the best tool for the job, and with that, comes learning a new language or framework. A lot of my experience thus far has been in 'App' development. I've done anything from android mobile apps, to HTML5 mobile webapps, to full HTML5 canvas hardware-accelerated mobile games. My passion comes in the form of user experiences, and designing great user interfaces. My favorite thing about front-end coding is the ability to go and see my code function in an entirely visual way, and try it out for myself until it feels right.
